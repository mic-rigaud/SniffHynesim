
\chapter{IDS}

\definition{IDS}{An intrusion detection system (IDS) inspects all inbound and
  outbound network activity and identifies suspicious patterns that may indicate
  a network or system attack from someone attempting to break into or compromise
  a system. \cite{webopedia}}
